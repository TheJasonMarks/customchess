# Privacy Policy for Custom Chess

**Last Updated: March 2025**

## Introduction

Custom Chess ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Photos and Images
- We access your device's photo library **only when you choose** to upload custom chess piece images
- Photos are stored locally on your device and on our servers to sync your custom pieces
- We do **not** share your photos with third parties

### Game Data
- Game progress, move history, and saved games
- Custom piece configurations
- App preferences and settings

### Analytics (Anonymous)
- App usage patterns to improve user experience
- Crash reports to fix bugs
- No personally identifiable information is collected

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide and maintain the game functionality
- To save your custom chess pieces and game progress
- To improve our app and user experience
- To process in-app purchases

## Data Storage

- Your data is stored securely on our servers
- Custom piece images are stored in encrypted format
- You can delete your data at any time by uninstalling the app

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **RevenueCat**: For processing in-app purchases
- **Google AdMob**: For displaying advertisements (free version only)

These services have their own privacy policies.

## Children's Privacy

Custom Chess is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Delete your data (by uninstalling the app)
- Opt-out of personalized ads (in device settings)

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
